Critera for data holders

  • Enable patients/members to view and download their records online

Critera for data receivers

  • Receive or transmit CCDA
  • App must be transparent with user about data security/privacy

Critera for supporting organizations

  • Take the Blue Button Pledge
  • Use the logo and phrase "Blue Button" to describe a way consumers can get electronic access to their health information through health IT. Please ensure that you follow the logo usage guidelines.
  • Help spread the word about Blue Button. Use our public service announcements and other assets for your outreach efforts. Blog, tweet #BlueButton, and use other social media to talk to consumers about the importance of Blue Button and consumer engagement.
