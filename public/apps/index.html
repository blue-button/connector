There are thousands of health apps out there.

Most of them require you to enter in your own information. Wouldn't it be nice if they connected directly to your information in your existing health records?

The apps here use a portfolio of nationally recognized standards including, but not limited to Blue Button+ standards, meaning they accept information directly from your healthcare providers' records or another data source.

  • 4Med Mobile Vitals Diary

    Our vitals tracking app allows patients to keep track of their health information and store it securely online and supports more than 100 wireless medical devices. Tracks Blood Pressure, Blood Glucose, Heart Rate, Weight, Temperature, Blood Oxygen, ECG, Spirometry and Medication Adherence.

  • Cake Health

    Brings all your health care plans together online so you can easily track your health spending — without the paperwork.
