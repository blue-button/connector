Criteria to be listed on the Blue Button Connector

For organizations to be listed under "Health Records"

  • Enable patients/members/customers to view and download their records online
  • Add your organization to the Connector.

For apps

  • Receive or transmit C-CDA or claims data
  • App must be transparent with user about data security/privacy
  • Add your app to the Connector
